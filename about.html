About Us

India’s No.1 Coaching Institute for Teaching & Leadership Exams

Study Portal is a professional coaching institute dedicated to preparing aspirants for teaching and educational leadership exams. Our focus centers on competitive exams that shape the future of education in India—TET, PRT, TGT, PGT, Principal & Vice Principal, Assistant Commissioner, and more across CTET, DSSSB, KVS, NVS, EMRS, UPSC, and state-level exams.

Who We Are

  • 12+ Years of Expertise in teacher and leadership exam coaching
  • 450+ Successful Selections in Principal & Vice Principal posts (KVS, NVS, UPSC, EMRS & State exams)
  • Thousands Guided: Aspirants mentored into teaching careers through PRT, TGT, PGT, TET exams
  • Nationwide Recognition for consistent results and trusted mentorship

Mission

To empower aspiring teachers, principals, and commissioners with the knowledge, skills, and strategies needed to crack competitive exams and achieve their dream careers.

Vision

To be India’s most trusted and result-oriented coaching hub for educators and educational leaders.

Why We Stand Out

We’re not just a coaching institute—we are career partners for educators and leaders. With a track record of consistent success, trusted teaching methods, and the highest number of selections, Study Portal is where aspirations turn into achievements.

“Your success is our mission.”

The Study Portal Way (Our Approach)

  • Focused Curriculum: Based on the latest exam pattern & syllabus
  • Expert Faculty: Trainers who know exams inside-out
  • Comprehensive Preparation: From written exams to interview batches
  • Proven Strategies: Preparation, revision, and time-management techniques
  • Personalized Mentorship: One-on-one guidance at every step
  • Result-Oriented Approach: 450+ leadership selections as proof
